The developer does not collect personal user data. No personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, device identifiers, or location is collected by the application.

The developer does not share user data with any third parties, companies, or organizations. Because the app does not collect any user data, there is no data shared with advertisers, analytics providers, or other services.
